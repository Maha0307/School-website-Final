    • SCHOOL UNIFORM

      All girl students shall adhere to dressing modestly in their uniforms. The uniform should be immaculately tailored, freshly laundered. Short pinafores, out-grown uniforms, ill fitted, faded or torn uniforms shall not be worn to school. Gold jewellery, anklets, hanging ear rings, fancy bangles, nail polish, make-up, fancy shoes / sandals / chappals, mehendi, growing long nails are prohibited in school. Long hair should be plaited in two plaits tied with clean ribbon over the shoulder, Hair of shoulder length should be tied with a ribbon. Hair upto the nape of the neck may be left loose.
